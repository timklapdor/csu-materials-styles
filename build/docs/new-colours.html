New Colours

A new colour palette has been developed to provide learning materials to utilise a wider colour palette than the faculty and brand colours. This is an acknowledgement of the importance of colour in creating visual flow and to improve and enhance the user experience from a usability and navigational perspective. The colours chosen have been designed with accessibility in mind and can be previewed here: Learning Material Colours. The colours are also provided with dark and light variations suitable for :hover states and a fade variations for light backgrounds.
